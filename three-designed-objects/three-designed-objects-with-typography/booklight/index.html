Book light

Like people do, I used to have trouble sleeping at some point in my life. Once I read a book on how to sleep, best tip I got was try to not look at screens for at least half an hour before bedtime.

Took me a little while to quit this habit but eventually I started to read books, just a few pages each night before bed. However the light in my room was a bit dim. So I got me this little book light and the trouble was gone.


  • a. Led

    It uses a led light. I’m far from being an electrician but I presume these things consume little energy. There’s also a choice of colors, you’ve got a warm light which is orange-y and a cold one which is white, currently displayed.

  • b. Clip

    The clip is a great design choice – you can attach it to any book you like. Boom!

  • c. Flexible stem

    Again this is good design because the light can be adjusted easily with this stem.


  • d. Charging port

    Fortunately it doesn’t run on batteries. You can use a typical mini usb cable to charge it up.
